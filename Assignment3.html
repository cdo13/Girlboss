Steps To Be A Good Rolemodel

  1. Be comfortable in your own skin
  2. Dress to impress
  3. Always check on your friends
  4. Make sure everyone is good and YOU are good

Being the life of the party doesn't mean you will be a mean and annoying person that doesn't care about anyone but themselves.
Being the life of the party means being an individual who is liked and looked up to, someone who is pretty/handsome that uplists others around them, someone that aspires to make everyone happy.

Take Example From The Famous

Making her to apply for Havard Law only to meet Warner again, however she was disappointed at how disrespectful Warner and her environment was. Recollection of What we have Learned

Elle woods possessed qualities listed below that helped her to be the main character whereever she went:

  • She was determined
  • She was charismatic
  • She was pretty and took care of herself
  • She was very caring of the people around her
  • She was smart and used her resourses well
  • She knew her limits and asked for help from her friends and role models
